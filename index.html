About $BINGO

$BINGO combines the appeal of meme coins with the cultural resonance and lore of "Bingo was his name-O", "Ol' MacDonald", and the symbolic power of Gematria (396). By integrating micro-tipping, NFT utility, and gamified community engagement, $BINGO is aiming for the XRP community's enthusiasm and adoption on platforms like X (#BINGO, #EIEIO, @bingoxrpl). Inspired by other XRPL meme tokens, $BINGO introduces unique tokenomics to provide long-term value and community loyalty.
